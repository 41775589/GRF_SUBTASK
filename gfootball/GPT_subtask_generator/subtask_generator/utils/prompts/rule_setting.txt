The rules are similar to the standard football rules.These include goal kicks, side kicks, corner kicks, both yellow and red cards, offsides, handballs and penalty kicks.
The agents are initialized in different locations on the map.
The actions available to an individual agent (player) are:
moving in eight directions: Top, Bottom, Left, Right, Top-Left, Top-Right, Bottom-Left, Bottom-Right
pass: Short Pass, High Pass, Long Pass
Shot, Do-Nothing, Sliding, Dribble, Stop-Dribble, Sprint, Stop-Moving, Stop-Sprint
